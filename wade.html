Dwyane Wade"The Way Of Wade/The Flash Point"

Dwyane Wade, born January 17, 1982 in Chicago, Illinois, is an American basketball player who played as a fullback in the National Basketball Association (NBA). With the Miami Heat franchise, he won the NBA title in 2006, 2012, and 2013 being awarded the title of best player in the finals at the 2006 Finals. He also won an Olympic champion title at the Olympic Games in summer of 2008 in Beijing. He had been training since the 2010-2011 season, with LeBron James (before his departure) and Chris Bosh, one of the best Big Three in the NBA. Wade became in March 2016 one of six players still active in the NBA to have scored more than 20,000 career points (along with LeBron James, Dirk Nowitzki, Vince Carter, Tony Parker and Carmelo Anthony). He is an emblematic player in the NBA, he left Miami in the summer of 2016 for the Chicago Bulls before joining the Cleveland Cavaliers of his friend LeBron James during the summer of 2017 before returning to the Miami Heat on the day of the trade deadline in 2018. He is also the Heat's statistical leader in points scored, assists, interceptions in the Florida franchise. On April 16, 2021, Dwyane Wade becomes a shareholder of the Utah Jazz
